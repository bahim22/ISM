Our founding

January 2021 | We started with the idea of helping connect Pittsburgh businesses with their intended, local target market. We have implemented various changes and have developed a sound business plan and are working from the ground floor in order to build a successful company.

Growth & beyond

Our Humble Pittsburgh Roots is only the beginning of our plans to scale. We will leverage our growth in order to help other minority-owned businesses flourish. We also plan on teaching local school-aged children business and technical skills by partnering with local businesses that want to help make a change in their community.
